# Trishabh Miraya - Visual North Star & Extraction

Based on the provided concept designs, we have extracted the following 10 key elements to serve as our creative benchmark. We will elevate these concepts to feel even more premium, utilizing our design skills (Taste, Impeccable, Emil Kowalski).

### 1. Hero Composition
**Extraction**: Split layout with massive typography on the left and a warm, cinematic image bleeding off the right edge. Two CTAs (Primary and WhatsApp).
**Evolution**: We will enhance this by ensuring the typography scaling is perfectly kerned (Impeccable) and introducing a slow, deliberate cinematic reveal of the hero image (Emil Kowalski) rather than a static load. 

### 2. Section Spacing
**Extraction**: Generous vertical rhythm separating distinct concepts (e.g., Intro Story vs. Well Designed Homes).
**Evolution**: We will rigorously apply an 8px-based spatial system (Taste Skill). Sections will breathe with up to 160px-200px of padding on desktop to create a true "editorial" pause, allowing the user to process the luxury.

### 3. Typography Hierarchy
**Extraction**: Bold, structured headings (Deep Emerald) contrasting with highly legible body text.
**Evolution**: Headings will utilize tight, negative letter-spacing for a modern, architectural feel. We will introduce a massive, unquestionable scale for key phrases like "EVERYTHING THAT MATTERS" to anchor the page emotionally.

### 4. Card Layouts
**Extraction**: Tall, structured vertical cards used in the Amenities section with solid color blocks at the bottom.
**Evolution**: Instead of flat cards, we will use delicate borders or extremely soft shadows (`rgba(0,0,0,0.04)`). The hover state will feature a smooth, cinematic color depth transition (Emil Kowalski) rather than a sudden jump or basic CSS scale.

### 5. Image Proportions
**Extraction**: Wide, panoramic architectural shots (Well Designed Homes) mixed with vertical lifestyle portraits.
**Evolution**: We will ensure images follow classic cinematic or editorial aspect ratios (16:9 for landscapes, 4:5 for portraits). Large panoramic images will break out of the 12-column grid to span edge-to-edge for dramatic impact.

### 6. CTA Placement
**Extraction**: Dual CTA strategy placed strategically at the end of storytelling arcs.
**Evolution**: Buttons will be refined with sharp corners or precise 2px rounding. The WhatsApp button will float globally, while primary CTAs will utilize a subtle lift and color inversion on hover to feel tactile and expensive.

### 7. Animation Opportunities
**Extraction**: Basic fade-ins, smooth parallax, image scaling, text reveals, and hover effects are suggested.
**Evolution**: Animations must be felt, not just seen. 
- Slow unmasking of images.
- Text blocks sliding up gently on scroll over a long duration (`0.8s - 1.2s`) using a custom cubic-bezier ease (`cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`).

### 8. Desktop Visual Balance
**Extraction**: Heavy reliance on asymmetrical layouts (Text Left / Image Right, or vice versa).
**Evolution**: We will push the 12-column grid further by offsetting text blocks by 1 or 2 columns to create tension. The balance will never be a perfect 50/50 split; it will always favor an editorial 60/40 or 70/30 weight.

### 9. Mobile Visual Balance
**Extraction**: Elements stack neatly; text followed by images.
**Evolution**: We will ensure mobile typography remains punchy but doesn't overwhelm the screen. The tall amenities cards and floor plans will be converted into smooth, snapping horizontal carousels to maintain vertical brevity and encourage interaction.

### 10. Overall Luxury Aesthetic
**Extraction**: Warm Ivory backgrounds, Deep Emerald accents, Gold highlights, and practical luxury messaging.
**Evolution**: The "Quiet Luxury" mandate. We will avoid pure white backgrounds entirely. We will remove any unnecessary UI borders, letting whitespace dictate the structure. It will feel less like a software product and exactly like a high-end, tactile digital brochure.
